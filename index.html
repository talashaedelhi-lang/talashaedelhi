About Our Heritage Walk

Talash-ae Dilli is a unique heritage walk experience designed to help people explore the hidden beauty, culture, and history of Delhi in a simple and engaging way. Our walks are carefully planned to take you through some of the most iconic and lesser-known places in the city. From ancient monuments to lively streets, every step tells a story. Our team of 50+ experienced guides shares interesting facts, local stories, and cultural insights that you won’t find in books. We focus on making the experience easy, fun, and memorable for everyone. Each walk lasts around 2 hours and includes historical exploration, storytelling, photography spots, and sometimes local food experiences. Whether you are a tourist or a local resident, our walks give you a fresh view of Delhi. We aim to connect people with the real soul of the city. Instead of just visiting places, you will understand their meaning, history, and importance. Join Talash-ae Dilli and discover Delhi in a way you have never seen before.
